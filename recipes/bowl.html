Low carb beef burrito bowl

Description

A flavorful yet light and healthy low-carb burrito bowl that is ready in under 30 minutes. It’s loaded with seasoned lean ground beef, cauliflower rice, avocado, tomato, lettuce and cheese. It’s great for meal prep too!

Ingredients

Beef

  • 500g lean ground beef
  • 1 onion
  • 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 1/4 tsp chili powder
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • salt, to taste
  • 1/2 tsp oil

Cauliflower rice

  • 1 small head cauliflower
  • salt, to taste
  • pepper, to taste
  • 1/2 tsp oil

Salad

  • 1 small head lettuce
  • 2 servings of guacamole
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 50g grated cheese
  • hot sauce (optional)

Steps

Prepare salad ingredients

  1. Finely chop lettuce and dice tomato.
  2. Make guacamole.

Prepare cauliflower rice

  1. Grate cauliflower into “rice” using a box grater. Alternatively, you can use a food processor for this.
  2. Heat up oil in a large nonstick skillet over medium heat. Add riced cauliflower, salt and pepper. Cook for 5-6 minutes or until desired tenderness.
  3. Remove from heat and set aside.

Prepare beef

  1. Small dice onion.
  2. Heat up oil in a large nonstick skillet over medium heat. Add onion and sauté until fragrant and transparent.
  3. Add ground beef, breaking it up with your spatula, and cook until browned.
  4. Add the rest of the ingredients for the beef and a splash of water. Stir to combine and continue cooking for 2-3 minutes.
  5. Remove from heat.

Assemble

  1. Divide lettuce between serving bowls. Then top with diced tomato, cauliflower rice, ground beef, cheese, guacamole and hot sauce (if using). Enjoy!
